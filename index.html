Healthier Homes. Healthier Lives.

Your doctor understands your body. Home Matter helps your doctor understand your home.

Home Matter Lab Test is an environmental home health assessment that evaluates key indoor environmental factors, including air quality, water quality, mold, allergens, hygiene, ventilation, moisture, chemicals, and surface contamination. The report helps doctors and families better understand environmental exposures within the home and supports informed decisions on environmental improvements and clinical care.

What We Test

A complete picture of your indoor environment

Five core categories, measured with approved devices and laboratory methods.

💨

Air Quality

  • PM2.5 & PM10
  • Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)
  • Total VOCs
  • Formaldehyde
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO)
💧

Water Quality

  • pH & TDS
  • Hardness
  • Residual Chlorine
  • Microbiological Analysis
  • Heavy Metals
🍄

Mold & Allergens

  • Mold Count (Air/Surface)
  • Aspergillus Species
  • Dust Mites
  • Pet Dander
  • Pollen Screening
🧫

Surface & Hygiene

  • ATP Hygiene Testing
  • Kitchen Surfaces
  • Bathroom Surfaces
  • Refrigerator Surfaces
  • Air Conditioner Vents
🏠

Indoor Environment

  • Ventilation (Airflow/ACH)
  • Noise Levels
  • Lighting (Lux)
  • Moisture & Dampness
  • Odor Evaluation
