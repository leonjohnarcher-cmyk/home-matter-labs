For Doctors

For Healthcare Professionals

Home Matter Lab Test provides additional information about a patient's home environment that may be relevant to overall health. It supports clinical discussions by presenting standardized environmental assessment findings alongside laboratory and device-based measurements.

Context for clinicians

Environmental context for the conversations you already have

Many health conversations touch on where and how a patient lives. The Home Matter Lab Test organizes environmental measurements into a consistent format, so you can consider home factors alongside your clinical assessment.

Findings are presented descriptively and are intended to support — not replace — your professional judgment and the care you provide.

Referrals

How to prescribe or refer a patient

1

Identify the need

Consider the Home Matter Lab Test when a patient's home environment may be relevant to their symptoms or care.

2

Prescribe the test

Recommend the assessment to your patient and select the package that best matches their situation.

3

Review the report

Receive the structured report and review the findings with your patient as part of their care.

The Report

What the doctor-friendly report includes

Standardized findings

Environmental measurements organized in a consistent, easy-to-scan structure.

Device & lab measurements

Approved device readings presented alongside laboratory results where applicable.

Guideline context

Findings reviewed against recognized regulatory guidelines to aid interpretation.
