How It Works

A clear, guided process

The Home Matter Lab Test is designed to fit naturally into your care, working alongside your doctor from referral to report.

1

Your doctor prescribes the test

Your doctor prescribes the Home Matter Lab Test when understanding your home environment may be helpful to your health. This keeps the assessment connected to your overall care and ensures the findings are reviewed in the right clinical context.

2

A detailed report is shared

Our team assesses the home using approved devices and laboratory methods. The results are compiled into a structured, doctor-friendly report that is shared with both the doctor and the family so everyone works from the same information.

3

Recommendations & follow-up

Where appropriate, the report includes environmental improvement recommendations and follow-up guidance. These practical steps help families act on the findings and support ongoing conversations with their healthcare provider.

FAQ

Common questions

The Home Matter Lab Test is designed to be prescribed by your doctor so that the findings can be reviewed alongside your overall health. We recommend speaking with your healthcare provider to book your assessment.

The on-site assessment is scheduled at a convenient time for your household. Some measurements are taken directly on-site, while certain samples are analyzed in the laboratory, which is reflected in your report timeline.

Once the assessment is complete, a structured report is shared with your doctor and your family. This allows your healthcare provider to review the findings with you and discuss any recommended next steps.
