About Home Matter Labs

An environmental health laboratory focused on helping people understand how their home environment may influence health and wellbeing.

Home Matter Labs is an environmental health laboratory focused on helping people understand how their home environment may influence health and wellbeing. By combining environmental assessment, laboratory testing, approved monitoring devices, and recognized regulatory guidelines, we provide structured reports that support healthier homes and informed healthcare decisions.

Our work is powered by Environmental Home Health Intelligence (EHHI™), a framework conceived and developed by G. Madan Mohan, who leads the research and innovation behind Home Matter Labs. EHHI™ brings together environmental measurements, laboratory findings, home characteristics, and occupant information into a standardized assessment that helps identify environmental exposures and supports health risk evaluation.

Home Matter Labs collaborates with healthcare professionals, accredited laboratories, environmental specialists, and technology partners to deliver consistent, high-quality home assessments. Our services are designed to support families across all life stages, including infants, children, pregnant women, older adults, and individuals living with chronic or complex medical conditions.

We believe that healthier homes contribute to healthier lives. Through scientific methods, quality standards, and continuous research, Home Matter Labs aims to advance environmental health awareness and provide practical insights that help families and healthcare professionals create safer indoor environments.
